The Framework

Five phases. One finished project.

DSIDR turns the messy process of doing something real into a clear path with an AI coach by your side.

D

Define your goal

Get specific about what you're building and what done looks like. No vague ideas — real, concrete outcomes.

S

State your why

Why does this matter to you? Your motivation is the fuel that keeps you going when things get hard.

I

Identify the landscape

Map your needs, opportunities, obstacles, and resources. Know what you're working with before you start.

D

Do the work

Break it into quests, find your time, track progress, and log updates. This is where it gets real.

R

Review and reflect

Look back at what you built, what you learned, and what you'd do differently. Then start the next one.
